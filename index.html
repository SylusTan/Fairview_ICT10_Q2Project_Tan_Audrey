About Our School

Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center is a progressive pre-k to 12 co-educational school, offering a viable alternative to international schools. We blend tradition and innovation to prepare a child for life.

Mission

To transform our students into self-developing and productive individuals who can succeed, direct, and influence the future of an ever-changing world.
